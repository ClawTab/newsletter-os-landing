How it works

From noise to newsletter in 4 steps

You stay in control. We handle everything else.

01 — DISCOVER

We scan the web

Our agents search X, Reddit, blogs, and academic papers for stories matching your niche. No RSS reader maintenance required.

→
02 — SCORE

Stories ranked by signal

Each story is scored on relevance, timeliness, and narrative fit. The best candidates surface automatically.

→
03 — REVIEW

You approve in one click

Review the curated stories and draft in a clean dashboard. Approve, reject, or edit — full control, zero friction.

→
04 — DISTRIBUTE

Multi-platform publish

One approval triggers email delivery + X thread. Both written in your voice, sent automatically.
